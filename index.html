Features

Interactive Gameplay

Experience engaging gameplay with intuitive button controls. Simply click on your choice (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) to play against the computer.

Real-Time Score Tracking

Stay updated on your progress with real-time scoring. Watch as your score and the computer's score dynamically update after each round, keeping you immersed in the game's intensity.

Exciting Outcome Notifications

Get instant feedback on each round's outcome. Receive clear and visually appealing notifications, declaring whether you've won, lost, or tied the round, enhancing the excitement of every decision.

About

Inspiration

This project was born out of a childhood fascination with the classic game of Rock Paper Scissors. Fond memories of playing with friends during school breaks inspired the creation of this digital rendition. As part of our journey at Holberton School, we embarked on this project to bring the joy of this timeless game to a new generation of players.
